## üíº Core Features & Functionality

### 1. Transaction Management

- **Invoice Creation:** Generate professional invoices with items, GST, discounts
- **Receipt Tracking:** Record money received (income/credit)
- **Payment Management:** Track money paid out (expenses/debit)
- **Purchase Orders:** Manage supplier purchases and inventory
- **Transaction History:** Complete audit trail of all financial activities

### 2. Customer & Supplier Management

- **Customer Database:** Store contact details, addresses, GST numbers
- **Supplier Directory:** Manage vendor relationships
- **Contact Integration:** Sync with phone contacts
- **Balance Tracking:** Real-time outstanding amounts
- **Transaction Ledgers:** Individual customer/supplier accounts

### 3. Financial Reporting

- **GST Summary:** Tax calculations and compliance reports
- **Cash Flow Analysis:** Income vs. expenses tracking
- **Daily Ledger:** Day-by-day transaction summaries
- **Customer/Supplier Ledgers:** Detailed account statements
- **Export Functionality:** CSV/PDF export for accounting software

### 4. AI-Powered Features

- **OCR Scanning:** Extract data from invoices, receipts, documents
- **Voice Input:** Speak transactions for hands-free data entry
- **Smart Parsing:** AI interprets natural language entries
- **Document Recognition:** Automatic categorization of financial documents

### 5. Advanced Capabilities

- **Document Attachments:** Link photos, PDFs to transactions
- **Folder Organization:** Custom categorization system
- **Multi-User Support:** Team collaboration with role-based access
- **Subscription Management:** Premium features and plan tracking
- **Push Notifications:** Real-time alerts and reminders
- **Offline Support:** Work without internet, sync when connected

### 6. Integration & Connectivity

- **CA Integration:** Direct connection to Chartered Accountants
- **Bank Reconciliation:** Link bank accounts for automatic matching
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Send invoices/receipts via messaging
- **Cloud Sync:** Secure data backup and multi-device access

## üé® Logo Design Requirements

### Design Principles

1. **Must Convey:**

   - ‚úÖ Intelligence & Automation (AI-powered)
   - ‚úÖ Financial Trust & Accuracy
   - ‚úÖ Modern Technology
   - ‚úÖ Business Professionalism
   - ‚úÖ Simplicity & Ease of Use

2. **Should Avoid:**
   - ‚ùå Overly complex designs
   - ‚ùå Outdated or traditional accounting imagery
   - ‚ùå Generic business icons
   - ‚ùå Cluttered or busy compositions

### Visual Elements to Consider

#### Symbol/Icon Concepts

- **Ledger/Book:** Traditional accounting ledger (modernized)
- **AI/Technology:** Circuit patterns, neural networks, smart elements
- **Financial:** Currency symbols, graphs, charts (subtle)
- **Connection:** Network nodes, sync symbols, integration
- **Growth:** Upward trends, progress indicators
- **Simplicity:** Clean lines, minimal forms

#### Typography Considerations

- **Font Style:** Modern, clean, professional
- **Weight:** Medium to Bold for impact
- **Readability:** Must work at small sizes (app icon)
- **Character:** Friendly but authoritative

### Technical Specifications

#### App Icon Requirements

- **Platforms:** iOS & Android
- **Sizes Needed:**
  - iOS: 1024x1024px (App Store)
  - Android: 512x512px (Play Store)
  - Various sizes: 48x48, 72x72, 96x96, 144x144, 192x192, 512x512
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Shape:** Square with rounded corners (iOS), adaptive icon (Android)

#### Logo Variations Needed

1. **Full Logo:** Icon + Text (horizontal and vertical)
2. **Icon Only:** Symbol/icon standalone
3. **Text Only:** Typography-based logo
4. **Monochrome:** Single color version
5. **Dark Mode:** Inverted for dark backgrounds
6. **Favicon:** Small web icon (if applicable)

#### Color Application

- **Primary:** Purple (#8f5cff) should be prominent
- **Supporting:** Can incorporate green (success) and red (warning) subtly
- **Versatility:** Must work on white, dark, and colored backgrounds
- **Accessibility:** Ensure sufficient contrast ratios

### Design Styles to Explore

1. **Modern Minimalist**

   - Clean, simple icon
   - Bold typography
   - Single color focus

2. **Tech-Forward**

   - Geometric shapes
   - Circuit/network patterns
   - Gradient effects

3. **Professional Classic**

   - Refined ledger/book icon
   - Elegant typography
   - Subtle sophistication

4. **Hybrid Approach**
   - Modern icon with classic elements
   - Balance of innovation and trust
   - Versatile application
